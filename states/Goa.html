The Parks of Goa

The Famous National Parks In Goa

1. Mollem National Park - Near Molem

Facilities

Restrooms: There are basic restroom facilities at the park's entry points.

Cafes: There are no major cafes within the park, but you can find small refreshment stalls.

Clinics: There are no medical facilities inside the park, but nearby towns have clinics and hospitals.

Hotels Available

Inside the Park: There are no hotels inside Mollem National Park.

Nearby: You can find accommodation options in nearby towns like Mollem and the surrounding areas, such as:

  • Hotel Nanutel in Ponda
  • Bambolim Beach Resort in Bambolim
  • The Fern Kadamba Hotel & Spa in Kadamba

Events Planned

Events are not regularly scheduled, but the park may host guided tours, nature walks, and educational programs on specific dates. Checking with local tourism offices or the park's official website can provide information on any upcoming events.

Activities Done

Visitors to Mollem National Park can engage in:

  • Wildlife Watching: Spot various species of birds, mammals, and reptiles.
  • Nature Trails and Hiking: Explore the park's diverse flora and fauna.
  • Bird Watching: The park is a haven for bird enthusiasts with numerous species.
  • Photography: The scenic landscapes and wildlife offer great opportunities for photography.

Famous For

Mollem National Park is renowned for its rich biodiversity and its role as a part of the Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary. It is known for its lush forests, waterfalls, and a variety of wildlife species.

Animals

What Animal Is It Famous For: The park is famous for sightings of the elusive black panther, although they are rare. It is also home to leopards, tigers, sloth bears, and various species of deer.

Plants/Vegetation/Climate

Vegetation: The park features dense evergreen forests with a rich variety of plant species, including teak, mahogany, and various tropical hardwoods.

Climate: It has a tropical monsoon climate, with heavy rainfall during the monsoon season and a warm, humid climate throughout the year.
