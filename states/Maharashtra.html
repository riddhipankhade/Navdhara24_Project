The Parks of Maharashtra

The Famous National Parks In Maharashtra

1.Chandoli National Park

Facilities

Restrooms: Available at various points within the park.

Cafes: Limited options; advisable to carry food and water.

Clinics: Basic first aid available; nearest hospitals are outside the park.

Visitor Centers: Information centers provide maps, guides, and other resources.

Hotels Available

Forest Guest Houses: Managed by the Forest Department, these offer basic amenities.

Private Lodges and Resorts: Several eco-friendly resorts and lodges are available around the park.

Events Planned

Wildlife Safaris: Regularly organized by the park authorities.

Bird Watching Tours: Special tours during the migratory season.

Nature Walks and Trekking: Organized periodically.

Activities Done

Wildlife Safaris: Jeep safaris to explore the wildlife.

Bird Watching: Rich avian diversity attracts bird watchers.

Trekking: Various trails for nature enthusiasts.

Camping: Designated areas for overnight camping.

Boating: Available at nearby reservoirs and rivers.

What it is Famous For

Koyna Dam Backwaters: A prominent feature in the park.

Scenic Beauty: Lush green landscapes and waterfalls.

What Animal it is Famous For

Indian Bison (Gaur): One of the key attractions.

Other Wildlife: Tigers, leopards, and sloth bears.

What Plant/Vegetation/Climate it is Famous For

Western Ghats Biodiversity: Dense forests with a variety of flora.

Climate: Tropical climate with heavy monsoon rains and a mild winter season.

Restaurants Available

Local Eateries: Available near the park boundaries.

Resort Dining: In-house dining options at nearby resorts and lodges.

Recommended Activities

Wildlife Safari: For an up-close experience with the park's fauna.

Bird Watching: Best during early mornings and late afternoons.

Trekking: Ideal for adventure enthusiasts.

Camping: Experience the wilderness overnight.

Photography: Great opportunities for landscape and wildlife photography.

2.Sanjay Gandhi National Park

Facilities

Restrooms: Available at various points within the park.

Cafes: There are several small cafes and food stalls within the park.

Clinics: Basic medical facilities and first aid are available within the park.

Hotels Available

Accommodation within the park: The park has some accommodation options like guest houses and cottages within its premises.

Nearby Hotels: Various hotels are available in the vicinity of Borivali, such as The E Hotel, GCC Hotel and Club, and Eskay Resorts.

Events Planned

Nature Walks and Workshops: Regularly organized for educational purposes.

Wildlife Photography Workshops: Conducted periodically.

Environmental Awareness Programs: Organized by various NGOs and the park administration.

Activities Done

Safari: Lion and tiger safari.

Boating: Available at the park's artificial lake.

Cycling: Rent bicycles and explore the park.

Trekking: Several trails are available, including the famous Kanheri Caves trek.

Bird Watching: The park is a haven for bird watchers with a variety of species.

What is it Famous For

Kanheri Caves: Ancient rock-cut caves dating back to the 1st century BCE.

Biodiversity: Rich flora and fauna, making it a popular destination for nature lovers.

Famous Animal

Leopards: The park is known for its leopard population.

Famous Plant/Vegetation/Climate

Flora: Includes teak, karanj, shisham, kadamba, and various other species.

Climate: Tropical climate with a monsoon season from June to September.

Restaurants Available

On-Site Cafes: Various small food stalls and cafes.

Nearby Restaurants: Several restaurants are available in Borivali, including The Fusion Kitchen, Urban Tadka, and Dakshinayan.

Recommended Activities

Safari Tours: To see lions and tigers in a semi-wild environment.

Trekking: Especially to the Kanheri Caves.

Boating: On the artificial lake for a peaceful experience.

Cycling: Exploring the park on a rented bicycle.

Bird Watching: Ideal for bird enthusiasts.

3.Navegaon National Park

Facilities

Restrooms: Available at various points within the park for visitor convenience.

Cafes and Restaurants: Basic eateries and cafes are available near the entrance and key spots within the park.

Clinics: First-aid facilities are available, but for advanced medical needs, visitors are advised to visit nearby towns.

Hotels Available

MTDC Resort: Managed by the Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation, this resort offers comfortable accommodation within the park.

Private Lodges and Guest Houses: Several private lodges and guest houses are available in the nearby towns of Navegaon and Gondia.

Events Planned

Regular wildlife safaris and bird watching tours.

Annual festivals and cultural events: Organized by local communities and park authorities.

Activities Done

Wildlife Safari: Guided tours to explore the rich wildlife of the park.

Bird Watching: The park is a paradise for bird watchers with numerous species to spot.

Boating: Boating in the park's lake is a popular activity.

Trekking: Various trekking trails are available for adventure enthusiasts.

Nature Walks: Guided nature walks to explore the flora and fauna.

Famous For

Animal: The park is famous for its large population of leopards and sloth bears.

Birds: A notable destination for bird watchers, especially during the migratory season.

Plant/Vegetation/Climate: Known for its diverse vegetation including mixed forest types and bamboo groves. The climate is typically tropical with a significant monsoon season.

Restaurants Available

Eateries and small restaurants: Available near the park entrance and in nearby towns.

Recommended Activities

Wildlife Safari: Essential for spotting leopards, bears, and other wildlife.

Bird Watching: Especially during the migratory season.

Boating: A serene experience on the park's lake.

Trekking and Nature Walks: Ideal for exploring the park's rich biodiversity.
