The Parks of Jharkhand

The Famous National Parks In Jharkhand

1. Betla National Park - Near Medininagar

Facilities

Restrooms: Basic restroom facilities are available within the park, particularly at key entry points and tourist centers.

Cafes: There are no major cafes inside the park. However, some local eateries and small food stalls may be present near the park’s entry points.

Clinics: Basic medical facilities are available in the nearby towns of Medininagar and Daltonganj. It’s advisable to carry any necessary medications with you.

Hotels Available

Several accommodation options are available near Betla National Park:

  • Betla Forest Rest House: Managed by the forest department, offering basic amenities and a close-to-nature experience.
  • Hotel Chotanagpur: Located in Daltonganj, providing more comfortable amenities.
  • Other local hotels and lodges: Various options are available in Medininagar and Daltonganj catering to different budgets.

Events Planned

  • Wildlife Safari: Regularly organized, with both jeep and elephant safaris available.
  • Bird Watching Tours: Seasonal tours focusing on the park's avian diversity.
  • Nature Camps: Occasionally organized for educational and recreational purposes.

Activities Done

  • Wildlife Safari: Explore the park via jeep or elephant safari.
  • Bird Watching: Spot numerous bird species.
  • Nature Walks: Guided tours through various trails.
  • Photography: Ideal for capturing wildlife and scenic landscapes.

What is it Famous For

Famous For: Diverse Wildlife: Betla National Park is renowned for its diverse wildlife, including tigers, elephants, and various other species.

Famous Animal

Animal: Elephants: The park is particularly famous for its population of Asian elephants.

Plant/Vegetation/Climate

Vegetation: The park features a mix of sal forests, bamboo groves, and grasslands.

Climate: The climate is tropical, with hot summers, a rainy monsoon season, and mild winters.

Restaurants Available

Restaurants: While there are no dedicated restaurants inside the park, local eateries in Daltonganj and Medininagar offer a range of food options.

Recommended Activities

  • Safari Tours: Experience the park’s rich wildlife through guided safaris.
  • Bird Watching: A must for bird enthusiasts, given the park's diverse avian population.
  • Nature Walks: Enjoy guided walks to explore the park’s flora and fauna.
  • Photography: Capture the natural beauty and wildlife of the park.
