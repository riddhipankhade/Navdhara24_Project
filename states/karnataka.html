The Parks of Karnataka

The Famous National Parks In Karnataka

1. Anshi National Park

Facilities

Restrooms: Basic restroom facilities are available within the park and at the visitor centers.

Cafes: There are limited food and beverage options within the park. It's advisable to carry your own food and water.

Clinics: There are no dedicated clinics within the park. Nearby towns or villages have medical facilities.

Hotels Available

Eco-Tourism Lodges: There are several eco-tourism lodges and forest guesthouses managed by the Karnataka Forest Department. Booking in advance is recommended.

Nearby Towns: In nearby towns like Karwar and Hubli, you can find various hotels and accommodations ranging from budget to mid-range.

Events Planned

  • Wildlife Tours: Regular wildlife safaris and guided tours are organized.
  • Bird Watching Camps: Occasional bird watching events are held.

Activities Done

  • Wildlife Safari: Jeep safaris through the park offer a chance to see various wildlife species.
  • Bird Watching: The park is a haven for bird watchers with numerous species.
  • Nature Walks: Guided nature walks provide an immersive experience of the park's flora and fauna.

What is it Famous For

Famous For: Tiger Reserve: It's famous for being a significant tiger reserve in Karnataka.

Biodiversity: The park is renowned for its rich biodiversity, including a variety of flora and fauna.

Famous Animal

Animal: Bengal Tiger: The park is known for its population of Bengal tigers, which is a major attraction.

Plant/Vegetation/Climate

Vegetation: The park features tropical dry deciduous forests, with a mix of grasslands and evergreen trees.

Climate: The climate is tropical with a significant monsoon season, leading to lush greenery during the rains.

Restaurants Available

In the Park: Limited food options are available, so it's best to carry your own supplies.

Nearby Areas: Restaurants and eateries can be found in nearby towns like Karwar.

Recommended Activities

  • Safari Tours: Explore the park's diverse wildlife through guided safari tours.
  • Bird Watching: Take advantage of the park's bird species by joining bird watching tours.
  • Photography: Capture the scenic landscapes and wildlife.
  • Nature Walks: Engage in guided walks to learn more about the park's ecosystem.

2. Bandipur National Park

Facilities

Restrooms: Available at main entry points and visitor centers.

Cafes: Basic refreshment options are available in the park, but more diverse options can be found outside the park in nearby towns.

Clinics: Basic medical facilities are available at the park's entrance and nearby towns. For more serious issues, you may need to travel to larger towns or cities.

Hotels Available

Inside the Park: Limited options, generally eco-lodges and forest rest houses.

Outside the Park: Several hotels and lodges in nearby towns like Gundlupet and Mysuru, offering various levels of comfort and amenities.

Events Planned

Events can vary annually and are often related to wildlife conservation, nature awareness programs, and special tours. It’s best to check with the park authorities or their website for current event schedules.

Activities Done

  • Safari Tours: Jeep and elephant safaris to spot wildlife.
  • Bird Watching: The park is a haven for bird enthusiasts.
  • Nature Walks: Guided walks to explore the flora and fauna.
  • Photography: Excellent opportunities for wildlife and landscape photography.

What is it Famous For

Famous For: Bandipur National Park is famous for its rich biodiversity and is a part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve. It is known for its conservation efforts and is a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts.

Famous Animal

Animal: The park is well-known for its population of tigers and elephants. It also has a significant number of leopards, gaur (Indian bison), and sambar deer.

Plant/Vegetation/Climate

Vegetation: The park features a mix of dry deciduous forests, scrublands, and grasslands.

Climate: Bandipur experiences a tropical climate with hot summers, a monsoon season, and mild winters.

Restaurants Available

Inside the Park: Limited dining options, mainly at lodges or forest rest houses.

Outside the Park: Numerous restaurants and eateries in nearby towns like Gundlupet and Mysuru offering a variety of cuisines.

Recommended Activities

  • Wildlife Safaris: To see tigers, elephants, and other wildlife.
  • Bird Watching: The park hosts a variety of bird species.
  • Nature Walks and Trekking: For a more immersive experience with nature.
  • Photography: Capture the stunning landscapes and wildlife.

3. Bannerghatta National Park

Facilities

Restrooms: Available at various points within the park, including the entrance and around major tourist spots.

Cafes: There are a few cafes and food stalls within the park where you can purchase snacks and beverages.

Clinics: There is a first aid station at the park entrance and basic medical facilities are available in nearby areas.

Hotels Available

In the Vicinity: There are several hotels and lodges around the park, including:

  • Eagle Ridge Resort: Located close to the park and offers a peaceful stay.
  • Our Native Village: A resort providing an eco-friendly experience.
  • Radha Hometel: A comfortable option in the nearby city of Bangalore.

Events Planned

Seasonal Events: The park hosts various events, including wildlife awareness programs and educational tours, particularly during special days like World Wildlife Day or Earth Day. Check the park’s official website or local tourism boards for the latest event schedules.

Activities Done

  • Safari Rides: Jeep safaris and bus safaris are available for wildlife viewing.
  • Butterfly Park: A dedicated area to explore diverse butterfly species.
  • Zoo Visit: The park houses a zoo with various animals, including tigers, lions, and elephants.
  • Nature Trails: Guided nature walks are available to explore the flora and fauna.

What is it Famous For

Famous For: The park is renowned for its diverse wildlife and the Bannerghatta Biological Park, which includes a zoo, butterfly park, and safari rides.

Famous Animal

Animal: It is particularly famous for its tiger and lion populations.

Plant/Vegetation/Climate

Vegetation: The park features a mix of deciduous forest and grassland.

Climate: The climate is typically tropical, with a monsoon season bringing heavy rains.

Restaurants Available

Available: There are a few food outlets within the park. For a broader range of dining options, you might need to venture to nearby Bangalore, which offers numerous restaurants and cafes.

Recommended Activities

  • Wildlife Safari: Essential for spotting various animals including tigers, lions, and elephants.
  • Butterfly Park Visit: Ideal for those interested in entomology and enjoying colorful butterflies.
  • Nature Trails: Great for hiking enthusiasts and nature lovers.
  • Zoo Tour: A chance to see a variety of animals and learn about wildlife conservation efforts.
