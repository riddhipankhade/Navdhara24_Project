The Parks of Gujarat

The Famous National Parks In Gujarat

1. Blackbuck National Park - Near Bhavnagar, Vallabhipur, Dholera, Dhandhuka

Facilities

Restrooms: Basic restroom facilities are available at the park’s entry points.

Cafes: There are no major cafes inside the park; however, you can find some local food stalls at the entrance.

Clinics: Medical facilities are limited. It’s advisable to carry any necessary medications and seek medical help in nearby towns if needed.

Hotels Available

Velavadar: Limited accommodation options are available in the nearby town of Velavadar.

Bhavnagar: For more choices, Bhavnagar (about 40 km away) offers a range of hotels, from budget to luxury.

Dhandhuka and Dholera: These towns also have some lodging options, though they are a bit farther away.

Events Planned

Seasonal Events: The park might have special events or wildlife viewing activities during peak tourist seasons. Check with the local authorities or park management for up-to-date information on events.

Activities Done

Visitors to Blackbuck National Park can engage in:

  • Wildlife Safari: The primary activity is wildlife safari, where you can observe blackbucks and other wildlife in their natural habitat.
  • Bird Watching: The park is also a good spot for birdwatching, with various species of migratory and resident birds.
  • Nature Walks: Guided nature walks are available to explore the park’s diverse flora and fauna.

Famous For

Blackbuck National Park is renowned for its population of blackbucks, which are a major attraction. The unique grassland ecosystem, including a variety of grasses and herbaceous plants, is another highlight.

Animals

Blackbuck: The Blackbuck National Park is specifically famous for its blackbuck population, which is one of the last strongholds of this species in India.

Plants/Vegetation/Climate

Vegetation: The park is characterized by its semi-arid grasslands, with a variety of grasses and shrubs. It does not have dense forests but features open grasslands.

Climate: The climate is typically dry and semi-arid, with hot summers, mild winters, and minimal rainfall.

Restaurants Available

Inside the Park: Limited options; basic snacks might be available at small stalls.

Nearby Areas: Restaurants and dining options can be found in Velavadar, Bhavnagar, Dhandhuka, and Dholera.

Recommended Activities

Wildlife Safaris: Early morning and late afternoon safaris are ideal for spotting blackbucks and other wildlife.

Photography: The park offers excellent opportunities for wildlife and landscape photography.

Bird Watching: Explore the diverse bird species that inhabit or migrate through the park.

Cafes: There are no major cafes inside the park; however, you can find some local food stalls at the entrance.

Hotels Available

Tulsishyam: Various lodging options are available in Tulsishyam, including budget and mid-range hotels.

Talala and Gir Gadhada: These towns also have lodging options, from budget accommodations to more comfortable stays.

Events Planned

Seasonal Events: The park might have special events or wildlife viewing activities during peak tourist seasons. Check with the local authorities or park management for up-to-date information on events.

Activities Done

Animals

3. Marine National Park, Gulf of Kutch - Near Dwarka, Bet Dwarka, Okha

Facilities

Restrooms: Basic restrooms are available at key points, but it's a good idea to check with local guides or your accommodation for specific locations.

Cafes: There are limited dining options within the park itself. Most visitors bring their own food or rely on local eateries in nearby towns.

Clinics: Basic medical facilities can be found in nearby towns like Dwarka and Okha. It's advisable to carry a first aid kit for minor issues.

Hotels Available

In Dwarka: Several hotels and guesthouses are available, ranging from budget to mid-range. Options include Hotel Sukh Sagar, Hotel Dwarkadhish, and Hotel Krushna.

In Okha: You can find accommodations like Hotel Shree Sairam and other budget-friendly options.

Near Bet Dwarka: Limited options, but some basic accommodations and guesthouses are available.

Events Planned

Specific events: Events might vary by season. Common events include local festivals and wildlife observation tours. It’s best to check with local tourism offices for up-to-date information.

Activities Done

  • Snorkeling: Popular for exploring coral reefs and marine life.
  • Boating: Boat tours are available to explore the park's coastline and nearby islands.
  • Wildlife Watching: Spotting various marine species, birds, and sometimes even dolphins.
  • Beach Walks: Enjoy the scenic beauty and tranquility of the park's beaches.

Famous For

Marine Biodiversity: The park is renowned for its rich marine life, including coral reefs, seagrass beds, and a variety of fish species.

Coral Reefs: One of the key attractions, known for their vibrant colors and diversity.

Animals

Dolphins: The park is known for occasional dolphin sightings.

Various Fish Species: Including reef fish and pelagic species.

Plants/Vegetation/Climate

Mangroves and Seagrass Beds: Important for marine ecology, providing habitat and food for various species.

Coral Reefs: Diverse and colorful, providing critical ecosystem services.

Climate: Typically hot and dry, with a semi-arid climate.

Restaurants Available

In Dwarka: Local eateries such as Shree Khodiyar Restaurant and Maruti Restaurant offer traditional Indian cuisine.

In Okha: Limited dining options, but some local restaurants serve regional dishes.

In Bet Dwarka: Basic local food options available, often in guesthouses or small eateries.

Recommended Activities

Coral Reef Exploration: Snorkeling or diving to explore the vibrant underwater ecosystems.

Boat Tours: To view marine life and scenic views of the park.

Wildlife Watching: Keep an eye out for unique species and enjoy birdwatching.

Cultural Tours: Explore local culture and traditions in nearby towns like Dwarka and Okha.
