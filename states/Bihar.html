The Parks of Bihar

The Famous National Parks In Bihar

1. Valmiki National Park - Near Valmiki Nagar, Bagaha

Facilities

Restrooms: Available at the entrance and several key locations within the park.

Cafes and Restaurants: Small cafes and food stalls are available at the park entrance and certain picnic areas.

Clinics: Basic first aid facilities are available at the park office. For serious medical issues, the nearest hospital is in Bagaha.

Hotels Available

  • Valmiki Vihar: A government-run guest house located near the park.
  • Eco Huts: Located within the park, these huts offer basic amenities for visitors.
  • Private Hotels and Resorts: Several private hotels and resorts are available in Valmiki Nagar and Bagaha, offering a range of accommodations from budget to luxury.

Events Planned

Valmiki National Park hosts several events throughout the year, including:

  • Wildlife Safaris: Regularly organized, with both jeep and elephant safaris available.
  • Bird Watching Tours: Special tours organized during the migratory season.
  • Educational Tours: Conducted for students and researchers focusing on wildlife conservation.
  • Cultural Events: Occasionally, local cultural performances are held to promote the cultural heritage of the region.

Activities Done

Wildlife Safaris: Jeep and elephant safaris to explore the park's rich biodiversity.

Bird Watching: The park is a haven for bird watchers with numerous migratory and resident species.

Trekking: Various trekking trails are available for adventure enthusiasts.

Camping: Designated camping areas within the park.

Nature Walks: Guided nature walks to explore the flora and fauna of the park.

What is it Famous For?

Valmiki National Park is known for its rich biodiversity, including a wide variety of flora and fauna. It is also associated with the sage Valmiki, the author of the Ramayana.

Famous Animal

The park is famous for its population of Bengal tigers. Other wildlife includes leopards, elephants, deer, and various species of birds.

Plant/Vegetation/Climate

Vegetation: The park is characterized by its tropical and subtropical moist deciduous forests.

Plants: Notable plant species include sal, jamun, and bamboo.

Climate: The park experiences a tropical climate with hot summers, a monsoon season, and cool winters.

Restaurants Available

  • Local Eateries: Small restaurants and food stalls near the park offer local cuisine.
  • Hotel Restaurants: Many hotels and resorts in the vicinity have in-house restaurants serving a variety of dishes.

Recommended Activities

Wildlife Safari: A must-do to experience the rich wildlife of the park.

Bird Watching: Ideal for bird enthusiasts.

Trekking: For adventure lovers, exploring the trails within the park.

Nature Walks: To appreciate the diverse flora and fauna.

Camping: For an immersive experience in the wilderness.
