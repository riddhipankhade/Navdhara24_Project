The Parks of West Bengal

The Famous National Parks In West Bengal

1.Buxa Tiger Reserve

Facilities

Restrooms: Available at key entry points and some watchtowers.

Cafes: Limited options; most visitors bring their own food.

Clinics: Basic medical facilities are available within the reserve. For more serious health issues, visitors need to go to nearby towns like Alipurduar or Siliguri.

Hotels Available

Samsing: Located near the reserve, offering budget and mid-range options.

Lataguri: Another nearby area with various hotels, ranging from budget to luxury.

Jorpokhri Wildlife Sanctuary: Has a few basic accommodations.

Alipurduar: Provides more options for hotels, including budget, mid-range, and luxury choices.

Events Planned

Wildlife Safari Tours: Regularly organized for tourists.

Conservation Awareness Programs: Occasionally held to promote wildlife conservation.

Local Festivals: May coincide with visits, such as local tribal festivals which offer a glimpse into the region's culture.

Activities Done

Wildlife Safaris: Jeep safaris and guided tours to spot wildlife.

Bird Watching: The reserve is home to various bird species.

Trekking: Several trails are available for nature enthusiasts.

Photography: Ideal for wildlife and landscape photography.

What Is It Famous For

Buxa Tiger Reserve: Known for its population of Bengal tigers and rich biodiversity.

What Animal Is It Famous For

Bengal Tiger: The reserve is particularly famous for its Bengal tiger population.

Other Wildlife: Includes elephants, leopards, and various species of deer and primates.

What Plant/Vegetation/Climate Is It Famous For

Vegetation: Dense forest with tropical moist deciduous trees and evergreen forests.

Climate: Sub-tropical climate with a monsoon season. Summers are hot and humid, while winters are mild and pleasant.

Restaurants Available

Limited Options: Food services are mostly available at hotels or guesthouses. Local eateries in nearby towns like Alipurduar and Lataguri offer a variety of cuisines.

Recommended Activities

Tiger Sightings: Go on safaris to increase your chances of seeing tigers.

Bird Watching: Explore different habitats to spot a wide range of bird species.

Nature Walks: Enjoy guided nature walks to understand the local ecosystem.

Cultural Tours: Visit local villages to experience the culture and lifestyle of the indigenous tribes.

2.Gorumara National Park

Facilities

Restrooms: Basic restrooms are available at key entry points and visitor centers.

Cafes: There are a few local eateries and small cafes near the park's entry points, but options are limited within the park.

Clinics: Basic medical facilities are available in nearby towns. For serious emergencies, you might need to travel to Jalpaiguri or Siliguri.

Hotels Available

Gorumara Jungle Camp: Located near the park, offering immersive stay experiences.

Raimatang Eco Resort: A popular eco-friendly resort close to the park.

Forest Rest Houses: Managed by the West Bengal Forest Development Corporation (WBFDC).

Guesthouses and Homestays: Various options in the nearby town of Lataguri and surrounding areas.

Events Planned

Events: The park doesn't have specific annual events, but it hosts nature walks, bird-watching tours, and wildlife safaris. It's best to check with local authorities or tour operators for any special events or activities during your visit.

Activities Done

Jeep Safaris: Offered to explore the park's wildlife and diverse landscapes.

Elephant Safaris: A popular way to get closer to the wildlife, especially in the buffer zones.

Bird Watching: The park is a haven for bird enthusiasts with many species to observe.

Nature Walks: Guided walks are available to explore the park’s flora and fauna.

What It Is Famous For

Famous For: Gorumara National Park is renowned for its rich biodiversity, particularly its population of Indian rhinoceroses. The park is also known for its dense forests and scenic beauty.

What Animal It Is Famous For

Famous Animal: The Indian one-horned rhinoceros is the most notable resident of the park. Other wildlife includes elephants, tigers, and various deer species.

What Plant/Vegetation/Climate It Is Famous For

Vegetation: The park features a mix of tropical moist deciduous forests and grasslands. It has a diverse range of flora, including tall grasses, sal trees, and a variety of shrubs.

Climate: The climate is typically subtropical, with hot summers, a monsoon season with heavy rainfall, and mild winters.

Restaurants Available

Restaurants: Dining options are quite basic near the park. Most visitors opt for local eateries or resort-based dining facilities. In Lataguri, you'll find a few local restaurants offering regional cuisine.

Recommended Activities

Wildlife Safaris: Both jeep and elephant safaris are must-do activities.

Bird Watching: Bring binoculars and a guidebook for bird-watching.

Nature Photography: The park’s scenic landscapes and wildlife offer excellent photography opportunities.

Visit Lataguri: Explore the nearby town for local culture and additional dining options.

3.Jaldapara National Park

Facilities

Restrooms: Available at key entry points and some designated spots within the park.

Cafes: Limited. You might find basic refreshment stalls at the park's entry points, but it's a good idea to carry your own snacks.

Clinics: Basic medical facilities are available near the park entrance. For more comprehensive care, it's best to travel to nearby towns.

Hotels Available

Sajnekhali Lodge: A government-run facility providing basic amenities and proximity to the park.

Jaldapara Tourist Lodge: Another government-run option offering comfortable accommodations.

Private Hotels: Various options are available in nearby towns like Madarihat, such as the Resort Aranya, which offer different levels of comfort and amenities.

Events Planned

Events: Events at Jaldapara are typically centered around wildlife conservation and ecotourism. Specific events may include guided safaris and nature walks. Checking with local authorities or the park's official website for the latest updates on events is a good idea.

Activities Done

Jeep Safaris: The primary way to explore the park, offering a chance to see wildlife up close.

Elephant Rides: A popular activity that provides a unique perspective of the park.

Bird Watching: Jaldapara is home to a variety of bird species, making it a great spot for bird enthusiasts.

Nature Walks: Guided walks through designated trails are available for a closer look at the park's flora and fauna.

What Is It Famous For

Famous For: Jaldapara National Park is renowned for its population of the Indian one-horned rhinoceros, making it a significant site for rhino conservation. It's also known for its rich biodiversity and scenic beauty.

What Animal Is It Famous For

Famous Animal: The Indian one-horned rhinoceros. The park is one of the best places to see these majestic animals in their natural habitat.

What Plant/Vegetation/Climate Is It Famous For

Vegetation: The park features a mix of grasslands and dense forest, with a variety of flora including tall grasses, shrubs, and forest trees.

Climate: The climate is tropical and humid, with a monsoon season from June to September.

Restaurants Available

Within the Park: Limited food options; it's best to bring your own food or plan to eat in nearby towns.

Nearby Towns: Various local restaurants and eateries in towns like Madarihat offer regional cuisine.

Recommended Activities

Rhino Safari: A must-do activity to spot the Indian one-horned rhinoceros.

Elephant Safari: Provides a unique experience and a chance to explore areas that are otherwise inaccessible.

Bird Watching: Ideal for bird enthusiasts, with opportunities to see various species.

Nature Photography: Great for capturing the park's scenic beauty and wildlife.
