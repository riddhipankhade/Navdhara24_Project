The Parks of Jammu & Kashmir

The Famous National Parks In Jammu & Kashmir

1. Dachigam National Park - Near Srinagar

Facilities

Restrooms: Basic restrooms are available at the park's entrance and certain viewpoints.

Cafes: There are no dedicated cafes within the park, but there might be small refreshment stalls near the entrance.

Clinics: Medical facilities are limited; it's best to carry basic first-aid supplies. For serious issues, you'll need to travel to Srinagar.

Hotels Available

Nearby Hotels:

  • Hotel Hilltop: Offers comfortable stays with easy access to the park.
  • Hotel Signature: Known for its good service and proximity to major attractions.
  • Khyber Himalayan Resort & Spa: A luxury option with stunning views and high-end amenities.

Events Planned

Planned Events: Dachigam National Park does not typically have regular public events. However, special wildlife observation tours and educational programs may be organized by local agencies or conservation groups.

Activities Done

  • Wildlife Viewing: The park is excellent for observing wildlife, particularly the Hangul deer.
  • Nature Walks: Guided walks through the park's diverse landscapes.
  • Bird Watching: Many bird species can be spotted in the park.

What Is It Famous For

Famous For: Dachigam National Park is renowned for its conservation efforts for the critically endangered Hangul deer (Kashmir Stag).

What Animal Is It Famous For

Animal: The Hangul deer, which is the primary attraction of the park.

What Plant/Vegetation/Climate Is It Famous For

Vegetation: The park features a mix of temperate broadleaf forests, coniferous forests, and alpine meadows.

Climate: The climate is temperate, with cold winters and mild to warm summers. Snow can be seen in winter.

Restaurants Available

Nearby Restaurants:

  • Shamyana Restaurant: Offers local Kashmiri cuisine.
  • Lhasa Restaurant: Known for its Tibetan and Indian dishes.
  • The Chinar Restaurant: Provides a mix of regional and international cuisines.

Recommended Activities

  • Wildlife Photography: Capture images of the rare Hangul deer and other wildlife.
  • Trekking: Explore the diverse terrain through guided trekking tours.
  • Picnicking: Enjoy a meal in designated areas while soaking in the natural beauty.

2. Kishtwar National Park - Near Kishtwar

Facilities

Restrooms: Basic restroom facilities are available at some entry points, but they might be limited. It’s advisable to check for specific locations.

Cafes: There are no dedicated cafes within the park. Visitors usually need to bring their own food and drink supplies.

Clinics: Medical facilities are limited. It’s best to carry a basic first-aid kit and be prepared for emergencies. The nearest major medical facilities are in Kishtwar town.

Hotels Available

Kishtwar: There are several hotels in Kishtwar town where visitors can stay. Options range from budget to mid-range accommodations.

  • Hotel Hilltop: Offers comfortable rooms and basic amenities.
  • Hotel Rattan Palace: Known for its good service and convenient location.
  • Hotel Shree Palace: Provides a range of amenities and services for travelers.

Events Planned

Events: The park does not have a regular schedule of events. However, local festivals and cultural events in Kishtwar might coincide with your visit. Checking with local tourism offices can provide information on any special events or activities.

Activities Done

  • Trekking and Hiking: The park offers various trekking routes through its scenic landscapes.
  • Wildlife Viewing: Spotting diverse wildlife is a popular activity.
  • Camping: Permits are needed for camping; check with the park authorities for designated camping areas.

Famous For

Famous For: The park is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, including alpine meadows, dense forests, and rugged mountains. It is also known for its rich biodiversity and unique ecosystems.

Famous Animal

Animal: Musk Deer is one of the notable wildlife species found in the park. Other species include the Himalayan Black Bear and the Snow Leopard.

Plant/Vegetation/Climate

Vegetation: The park features a variety of plant species, including coniferous forests, alpine meadows, and medicinal plants.

Climate: The climate varies from temperate in the lower regions to alpine at higher elevations. Winters can be very cold with heavy snowfall, while summers are mild and pleasant.

Restaurants Available

Restaurants: There are no restaurants within the park. Visitors can find dining options in Kishtwar town before entering the park.

Recommended Activities

  • Exploring Wildlife: Engage in wildlife watching to observe the park’s diverse species.
  • Photography: Capture the stunning landscapes and unique wildlife.
  • Nature Walks: Enjoy serene walks through the park’s picturesque environments.

3. Salim Ali National Park - Near Srinagar

Facilities

Restrooms: Basic restroom facilities are available within the park.

Cafes: There are no major cafes or restaurants directly within the park, but nearby Srinagar offers various dining options.

Clinics: Medical facilities are not available within the park; visitors may need to travel to Srinagar for medical needs.

Hotels Available

Srinagar: There are numerous hotels in Srinagar, ranging from luxury to budget options. Some notable hotels include the Lalit Grand Palace, Hotel Srinagar, and Houseboat accommodations.

Events Planned

Events: The park does not typically host major events but may have occasional bird-watching tours or educational programs. For specific events, it’s best to check with local tour operators or the park administration.

Activities Done

  • Bird Watching: The park is renowned for its diverse bird species, making it a hotspot for bird watchers.
  • Nature Walks: Guided nature walks and trails offer a chance to explore the park’s flora and fauna.
  • Photography: The scenic beauty and wildlife make it a popular spot for photography.

What is it Famous For

Famous For: Bird Diversity: It is famous for being a haven for various bird species, including endangered ones. It’s named after the renowned ornithologist Dr. Salim Ali.

What Animal is it Famous For

Animal: Birds: The park is particularly famous for its bird species, including the Himalayan monal, the Kashmir flycatcher, and the golden oriole.

What Plant/Vegetation/Climate is it Famous For

Vegetation: The park features dense riparian forests, with a mix of broadleaf and coniferous trees.

Climate: It has a temperate climate with cool summers and cold winters. The area is lush and green, especially in the spring and summer months.

Restaurants Available

Restaurants: While there are no restaurants within the park, Srinagar offers a variety of dining options. Some recommended places in Srinagar include:

  • Mughal Darbar
  • Shamyana Restaurant
  • Ahdoos Restaurant

Recommended Activities

  • Bird Watching Tours: Engage in organized bird-watching tours to spot various avian species.
  • Nature Photography: Capture the park’s stunning landscapes and wildlife.
  • Guided Nature Walks: Explore the park’s natural beauty with the help of a guide.
