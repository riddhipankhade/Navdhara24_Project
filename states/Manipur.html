The Parks of Manipur

The Famous National Parks In Manipur

1.Keibul Lamjao National Park

Facilities

Restrooms: Basic restroom facilities are available at the park entrance and certain points within the park.

Cafes: There are no cafes within the park itself, but there are small eateries and tea stalls in the nearby villages.

Clinics: There are no clinics within the park. The nearest medical facilities are in Moirang, which is about 7-10 km away from the park.

Hotels Available

Loktak Aquamarine: Located near Loktak Lake, it offers a comfortable stay with beautiful views.

Classic Hotel, Imphal: A bit further away in Imphal, it provides modern amenities and services.

Hotel Imphal: Another option in Imphal, offering decent accommodation and dining facilities.

Events Planned

Wildlife Week: Celebrated in the first week of October, it includes various awareness programs and activities related to wildlife conservation.

Bird Watching Events: Occasionally organized, especially during the migratory bird season.

Activities Done

Boat Rides: Explore Loktak Lake and its floating phumdis (heterogeneous mass of vegetation, soil, and organic matter).

Bird Watching: A haven for bird watchers, especially during the migratory season.

Wildlife Safaris: Guided tours to spot the endangered Sangai deer and other wildlife.

Nature Walks: Guided walks through the park to explore its unique flora and fauna.

What is it Famous For

Floating Phumdis: Unique floating biomass in Loktak Lake.

Sangai Deer: The endangered brow-antlered deer, which is also the state animal of Manipur.

Animal it is Famous For

Sangai Deer: Also known as the dancing deer due to its delicate movements, it is endemic to this region.

Plant/Vegetation/Climate

Vegetation: The park is characterized by its phumdi vegetation, a mix of aquatic and semi-aquatic plants.

Climate: Subtropical monsoon climate with moderate to heavy rainfall during the monsoon season.

Restaurants Available

Local Eateries: Small local eateries are available in the nearby villages and towns, offering Manipuri cuisine.

Hotel Restaurants: Restaurants in nearby hotels like Loktak Aquamarine and those in Imphal offer a variety of Indian and local dishes.

Recommended Activities

Boat Ride on Loktak Lake: Experience the unique floating islands and spot the Sangai deer.

Bird Watching: Ideal for bird enthusiasts, especially during the winter months.

Nature Walks: Explore the rich biodiversity of the park on foot with a guide.

Wildlife Photography: Capture the unique landscapes and wildlife of the park.

2.Shirui National Park

Facilities

Restrooms: Basic restroom facilities are available near the entrance and at key locations within the park.

Cafes: Limited cafes and food stalls can be found near the entrance and popular spots.

Clinics: No clinics within the park, but medical facilities are available in nearby towns.

Hotels Available

Nearby Hotels:

Hotel Nirmala: Located in Ukhrul town, offering comfortable stays with basic amenities.

Haven Hotel: Known for its hospitality and proximity to the park.

Phungreitang Tourist Home: A government-run facility with basic accommodations.

Events Planned

Shirui Lily Festival: Celebrated annually in May, highlighting the bloom of the rare Shirui Lily.

Cultural Events: Traditional dance and music performances by local tribes during the festival.

Activities Done

Trekking: Various trekking routes with different difficulty levels.

Bird Watching: The park is a haven for bird watchers.

Photography: Ideal for nature and wildlife photography.

Nature Walks: Guided tours to explore the flora and fauna.

What is it Famous For

Shirui Lily (Lilium mackliniae): A rare and endangered flower that blooms only in this region.

Cultural Heritage: Rich traditions and customs of the Tangkhul Naga tribe.

What Animal is it Famous For

Blyth's Tragopan: A rare bird species found in the park.

Leopard Cat: Among the diverse wildlife inhabiting the park.

What Plant/Vegetation/Climate is it Famous For

Shirui Lily: The park's unique feature.

Vegetation: Sub-tropical and temperate forests with rich biodiversity.

Climate: Mild and pleasant, with cool summers and cold winters.

Restaurants Available

Local Eateries: Small restaurants and food stalls offering traditional Manipuri cuisine.

Hotel Restaurants: Dining facilities available within nearby hotels.

Recommended Activities

Trek to Shirui Kashong Peak: Offers breathtaking views and a chance to see the Shirui Lily.

Bird Watching Tours: Led by experienced guides.

Cultural Tours: Interact with local tribes and learn about their traditions.

Photography Excursions: Capture the scenic beauty and unique flora and fauna.
